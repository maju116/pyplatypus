PyPlatypus Creators

It is high time we introduced ourselves. We met while working on the R&D XAI-based project that was all about delivering the explainable automatic diagnosis of x-ray hip joint images. Thereafter we have been working together occasionaly fueled by the passion for the one and only Computer Vision with a focus on medical data. Our branching paths finally meets here and from their intersection the PyPlatypus was born.
